LEAVE POLICY
------------
Employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leave must be applied for at least 2 weeks in advance via the HR portal.
Unused leave of up to 10 days can be carried forward to the next calendar year.
Sick leave is separate from annual leave and is limited to 10 days per year with a medical certificate.

REMOTE WORK POLICY
------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
All remote workers must be available during core hours: 10 AM to 4 PM local time.
A stable internet connection of at least 10 Mbps is required for remote work.
Remote work privileges can be revoked if performance targets are not met.

EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
---------------------
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the expense being incurred.
Receipts are required for any expense above $25.
Travel expenses must be pre-approved by a department head for amounts exceeding $500.
Reimbursements are processed within 15 business days of submission.

CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------
All employees must treat colleagues with respect and professionalism.
Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated.
Confidential company information must not be shared with external parties.
Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.

IT SECURITY POLICY
------------------
Employees must use strong passwords of at least 12 characters.
Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all company accounts.
Personal USB devices may not be connected to company computers.
Any suspected security breach must be reported to IT within 1 hour of discovery.
